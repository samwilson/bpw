\chapter{ Family}


Mary Barton's parents were Richard Till Worthington (1875–1936) and Edith Marion Worthington (née Hedges).\cite{1904WorthingtonMarriage}
She was born in 1905.
Married BPW in about March 1943.
Died in 1990.
Buried at St Mary's in Erwarton, Suffolk. Her gravestone includes
``Medical practitioner North-West Frontier of India /
Consultant Gynecologist London /
Pioneer in fertility /
Her spirit and work live on.''\cite{BartonGravestone}
